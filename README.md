**Vision**
To reduce the burden on the gym trainers and provide support to all the gym members (clients*). Provide best-in-class service to their clients.

**Gym trainer role**
- Set an achievable target for a defined period. 
- Prepare a diet plan
- Prepare a workout plan and change it based on the progress.
- Educate your client during the process on what you are doing and why you are doing it.
- Making their clients regular & keeping them motivated.
- Supervising clients while the client is working out. Giving feedback during the session to get the maximum impact and avoid injuries.
- Resolve queries of the client.

**Product Solution**
- Client onboarding: Name, Email, Contact number, Plan period, Amount paid
- KYC: Age, Gender, Workout Experience, Health issues/ any past injuries, Diet constraint: (veg/non-veg, Lactose intolerant, supplement/without supplement, regular cuisine: north, gujju, South, north-east, etc.).
- Target: Understand the goal of the client. (Fat loss, Weight gain, Build muscle, Improve body posture)
- Prepare a diet plan considering the details filled in by the client.
- Prepare a weekly workout plan.
- Allowed cheat meals with frequency to have a cheat meal.
- Give explanations for whatever is prescribed.
- Keeping regular checks/ inputs from the client.
- Supervise Clients: Camera-enabled supervision, real-time feedback to the client (Audio+Text-assistant).
- Chat Assistant.
- Progress Report: Option to add weight and other details, Image etc.
